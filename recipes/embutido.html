Embutido Cordon Bleu

Cordon bleu with a Pinoy twist!

Here is a new noche buena dish that you will surely enjoy this Christmas. Two of our favorite dishes combined into one will surely delight everyone.

Recipe from panlasangpinoy.com

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices ham
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • store-bought or home made embutido
  • Dijon sauce

Steps

  1. Lay a sheet of foil on a flat surface. Scoop ¾ cups of meat mixture and spread it on the foil. Lay 2 slices of ham over the meat mixture and top it with 2 slices of cheese. Roll the foil and secure by twisting the ends.
  2. Steam for 1 hour. Remove from the steamer and let this cool down. Remove foil wrap.
  3. Heat 2 cups cooking oil.
  4. Dredge each embutido in all-purpose flour. Dip it in beaten egg, and then coat with panko breadcrumbs.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  6. Make the Dijon cream sauce by melting butter in a saucepan. Add onion. Cook until soft.
  7. Pour heavy whipping cream. Stir and add Dijon mustard. Stir and continue cooking using between low to medium heat setting until the sauce reduces to half. Add salt to taste.
  8. Arrange the embutido cordon bleu on a serving plate. Serve with creamy Dijon sauce. Share and enjoy!
